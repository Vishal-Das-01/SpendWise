SpendWise is an **AI-powered expense tracking and budgeting platform** designed to help international students manage their finances while living abroad.  
The application uses **multimodal AI** to automatically interpret receipts and natural language expense inputs, categorize spending, and provide budget insights through an interactive dashboard.

## ğŸ“Œ Problem Statement

International students often struggle with:
- Tracking daily expenses in unfamiliar currencies
- Manually categorizing spending
- Understanding where money is being spent
- Staying within monthly budgets

Traditional tools rely heavily on spreadsheets or manual entry. SpendWise removes this friction by using AI to automate expense logging and categorization.
